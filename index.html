About Joanna

Joanna Rostetter-Bohn (M.Ed) is the president of Fund For Equal Access to Education, which she co-founded with her late father Dr. David Rostetter. Joanna specializes in special education advocacy, working with parents and students to navigate the complexities of IEP and 504 plans.

With deep expertise in intervention strategies, learning disabilities, and educational rights, she ensures every student receives appropriate support and services.

Joanna also provides private tutoring in reading, writing, mathematics, executive functioning, and compensatory education claims. Her individualized approach meets students where they are, building confidence alongside academic skills.
